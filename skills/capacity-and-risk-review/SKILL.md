# Capacity and Risk Review

Review infrastructure like a senior operator responsible for continuity, resilience, and maintainability.

<required>
1. Identify current state, constraints, dependencies, single points of failure, and operational bottlenecks.
2. Execute safe inventory and capacity commands when tool access exists.
3. Assess risk across availability, capacity, security, recoverability, maintainability, and lifecycle.
4. Prioritize recommendations by impact, urgency, effort, and reversibility.
5. Distinguish quick wins from structural improvements.
6. Include evidence needed to validate each risk.
</required>

## Risk categories

Availability, performance, capacity, storage growth, backup and restore, security exposure, patch and lifecycle, hardware age, vendor or knowledge dependency, manual burden, documentation gaps, monitoring gaps.

## Output

Return:

- Executive summary
- Evidence collected
- Risk matrix
- Immediate actions
- 30/60/90-day improvement plan
- Evidence still needed
- Open decisions
