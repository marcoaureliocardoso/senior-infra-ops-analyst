# Runbook Template

A runbook is linear: follow these steps for a known operational task.

## Purpose

Describe the task, system, and success condition.

## Scope

List included systems and explicit exclusions.

## Prerequisites

Access, tools, backups, maintenance window, and approvals needed.

## Safety gates

State which steps are read-only and which require approval.

## Procedure

| Step | Command/action | Expected result | If abnormal |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 |  |  |  |

## Validation

Commands or checks that prove the task succeeded.

## Rollback or recovery

Steps to restore the previous state or escalate safely.

## Evidence to archive

Command records, outputs, approvals, screenshots, and ticket links.

## Escalation

Who to contact, when to stop, and what information to provide.
